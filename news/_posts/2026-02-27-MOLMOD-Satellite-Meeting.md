## The Romanian molecular modeling community is holding a workshop as a satellite meeting to ISQBP

The Molecular Modeling in Chemistry and Biochemistry Conference [MOLMOD](https://chem.ubbcluj.ro/molmod_2026) will take place right after the ISQBP President's meeting from Thursday 18.06 to Saturday 20.06.

For details please consult the [link](https://chem.ubbcluj.ro/molmod_2026)

Everyone attending the ISQBP President's meeting who is interested in this Romanian sattelite meeting is welcome to register for it and participate.
