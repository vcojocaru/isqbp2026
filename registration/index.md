Registration and abstract submission is now open. The registration fees include the access to all lectures and the banquet

### <span style="color:red">Please register [here](registration-form)</span>

***IMPORTANT NOTE***: For the payment of the registration fee, we highly recommend paying directly via the online payment platform available on the registration form. This makes the life of the organizers easier because the platform allows immediate tracing of the payments. However, if there is a very objective reason you are not able to pay via the online platform, please find at the end of this page the account information of the Babes-Bolyai university. If you have to pay by bank transfer, ***please include "ISQBP2026" in your payment reference/details field*** and ***please send us a proof of payment*** by email and include the translation number in the registration form.
